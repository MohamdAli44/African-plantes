    About

    Who we are

    We're a family-Agri-business, located in Fayoum-Egypt since 2012, to grow and produce a sustainable, holistic way for B2B

    • We specialize in Aloe vera growing in Egypt
    • Our products comply fully with ISO standards for food safety and quality. Your health and that of those around you is so important.
    • Our company substantially uses our farms to produce Aloe Vera and many herbal plants.

    Our idea is to offer mono-type plants as a raw material for all of the global supply chains of the medicinal, and cosmetic industries and international supermarket chains, to make a more sustainable business, and lifestyle quality. We believe in the power of herbal drugs, dried herbs, spices, food, and the significance of living a healthy life, and we want to partake in that with you.
